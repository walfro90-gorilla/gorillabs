# Product Overview

## Gorilla Labs

A bilingual (Spanish/English) software development company website serving the El Paso, Texas and Ciudad Ju√°rez, Mexico border region.

### Core Features

- **E-commerce Platform**: Shopping cart, checkout, order management for software services
- **Service Showcase**: Portfolio, blog, service listings with detailed pages
- **Admin Panel**: CRM, content management (blog, portfolio, services), SEO tools, chatbot configuration
- **User Accounts**: Authentication, profile management, order history
- **AI Integration**: Live chat, AI-powered project estimator
- **Visual Effects**: 3D graphics, particle systems, shader effects for impressive homepage

### Target Audience

- **Public/Visitors**: Browse services, portfolio, blog content
- **Authenticated Users**: Purchase services, manage account, track orders
- **Administrators**: Full content and business management access

### Key Business Goals

- Showcase technical expertise through impressive visual design
- Provide bilingual experience for border region market
- Enable online service sales with smooth checkout flow
- Demonstrate AI/ML capabilities through interactive features
